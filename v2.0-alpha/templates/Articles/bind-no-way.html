Setting an object from viewModel

You may wish to set the property of a viewModel, but not bind it. In this case you can use the "no binding" binding. To us no binding you must use the --nb .

<wo.content-control model="busybody.makeObservable({ value: 'Hello wipeout!' })"> <set-template> <wo.content-control model--nb="$this.model.value"> <set-template> Setter bindings: <span wo-content="$model"></span> </set-template> </wo.content-control> </set-template> </wo.content-control> <wo.view template-id="'bind-no-way-script-1'"></wo.view>
